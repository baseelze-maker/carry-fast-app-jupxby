
# Wasel App - Logo Design Brief

## App Overview
**Wasel** is a peer-to-peer delivery platform that connects travelers with people who need to send items. The app enables travelers to monetize their unused luggage space while providing senders with a fast, affordable, and personal delivery alternative to traditional courier services.

---

## Logo Design Concept

### Core Message
The logo should communicate:
- **Connection** - Bringing travelers and senders together
- **Trust** - Safe, reliable delivery service
- **Speed** - Fast, efficient delivery
- **Global Reach** - International travel and delivery
- **Simplicity** - Easy-to-use platform
- **Community** - People helping people

---

## Visual Elements to Consider

### Primary Concepts

**1. Travel & Movement**
- Stylized airplane silhouette
- Luggage or suitcase icon
- Flight path or trajectory line
- Globe with travel routes
- Map pin or location marker
- Passport or boarding pass elements

**2. Connection & Exchange**
- Interlocking shapes or hands
- Bridge or link symbol
- Two points connected by a line
- Network nodes
- Handshake or exchange gesture
- Package passing between people

**3. Delivery & Package**
- Box or package icon
- Envelope or document
- Delivery arrow
- Checkmark (successful delivery)
- Shield (secure delivery)

### Recommended Symbol Combinations
- **Airplane + Package**: Shows travel-based delivery
- **Two Location Pins Connected**: Represents origin and destination
- **Globe + Arrow**: International delivery
- **Luggage + Heart**: Community-driven, caring service
- **Interlocking Circles**: Connection between travelers and senders

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors (from app design)

**Green (#4CAF50)** - Primary Brand Color
- Represents: Growth, trust, success, go/proceed
- Usage: Main logo color, primary buttons, success states
- Psychology: Evokes reliability and positive action

**Blue (#2196F3)** - Secondary Brand Color
- Represents: Trust, professionalism, sky/travel, communication
- Usage: Secondary elements, accents, links
- Psychology: Creates sense of security and dependability

**Amber/Yellow (#FFC107)** - Accent Color
- Represents: Energy, optimism, speed, value
- Usage: Highlights, call-to-action elements, notifications
- Psychology: Draws attention, suggests affordability and opportunity

### Supporting Colors

**White (#FFFFFF)** - Card/Clean Background
- Usage: Logo background, negative space, clean layouts

**Dark Gray (#333333)** - Primary Text
- Usage: Text elements in logo, wordmark

**Light Gray (#F5F5F5)** - Background
- Usage: App background, subtle elements

### Color Combinations for Logo

**Option 1: Green Primary**
- Main icon: Green (#4CAF50)
- Accent details: Blue (#2196F3)
- Text: Dark Gray (#333333)
- Best for: Trust and reliability emphasis

**Option 2: Blue Primary**
- Main icon: Blue (#2196F3)
- Accent details: Green (#4CAF50)
- Text: Dark Gray (#333333)
- Best for: Professional, travel-focused look

**Option 3: Gradient**
- Gradient from Blue to Green
- Text: Dark Gray (#333333)
- Best for: Modern, dynamic feel

**Option 4: Dual-Tone**
- Left half: Blue (traveler)
- Right half: Green (sender)
- Text: Dark Gray (#333333)
- Best for: Emphasizing the two-sided marketplace

## Typography Recommendations

### Wordmark Style

**Modern Sans-Serif Fonts:**
- Clean, readable, contemporary
- Suggests efficiency and simplicity
- Examples: Montserrat, Poppins, Inter, Nunito

**Rounded Sans-Serif:**
- Friendly, approachable, community-focused
- Suggests warmth and trust
- Examples: Quicksand, Comfortaa, Varela Round

**Bold & Confident:**
- Strong, reliable, trustworthy
- Suggests security and professionalism
- Examples: Raleway Bold, Roboto Bold, Open Sans Bold

### Text Treatment
- **"Wasel"** should be clear and legible at all sizes
- Consider lowercase for friendly feel: "wasel"
- Or title case for professional feel: "Wasel"
- Optional tagline: "Travel. Deliver. Connect." or "Delivery Made Personal"

- Clean lines

### Friendly & Approachable

- Warm, inviting feel
- Not too corporate or cold

### Trustworthy & Professional
- Balanced composition
- Stable, grounded design
- Quality craftsmanship

### Memorable & Unique
- Distinctive shape
- Easy to recognize

## Technical Specifications

### File Formats Needed
- **Vector**: SVG, AI, EPS (for scaling)
- **Raster**: PNG with transparency (various sizes)
- **App Icon**: 1024x1024px PNG
- **Favicon**: 32x32px, 16x16px

### Size Requirements
- App icon: 1024x1024px (iOS), 512x512px (Android)
- Splash screen: 2048x2048px
- Social media: 400x400px (profile), 1200x630px (cover)
- Website: Various responsive sizes

### Clear Space
- Minimum clear space around logo: Equal to height of "W" in wordmark
- No other elements should intrude into this space

### Minimum Size
- Logo should remain legible at 24px height
- Icon-only version should work at 16px

## Design Process Recommendations

### Step 1: Sketching
- Create 20-30 rough concept sketches
- Explore different visual metaphors
- Don't commit to details yet

### Step 2: Digital Refinement
- Select top 3-5 concepts
- Create clean digital versions
- Test at different sizes

### Step 3: Color Application
- Apply color palette to refined concepts
- Create variations with different color combinations
- Test on light and dark backgrounds

### Step 4: Typography
- Pair icon with wordmark
- Test different font options
- Ensure readability at all sizes

### Step 5: Refinement
- Get feedback from stakeholders
- Make adjustments
- Create final variations

### Step 6: Finalization
- Create all required file formats
- Prepare brand guidelines document
- Deliver complete logo package

## Additional Notes

### Cultural Considerations
- Ensure design works internationally
- Avoid symbols that may have negative connotations in certain cultures
- Test with diverse audience

### Accessibility
- Ensure sufficient contrast for visibility
- Consider colorblind-friendly palette
- Test readability for users with visual impairments

### Future-Proofing
- Design should remain relevant for 5-10 years
- Avoid overly trendy elements
- Allow for minor updates without complete redesign

## Summary

The Wasel logo should be a modern, trustworthy symbol that represents the connection between travelers and senders. Using the app's color palette of green (primary), blue (secondary), and amber (accent), the logo should incorporate travel and delivery elements in a clean, scalable design. The final logo must work across all platforms and sizes while communicating trust, efficiency, and community.

**Key Visual Elements**: Travel (airplane/luggage) + Delivery (package) + Connection (linking elements)

**Key Colors**: Green (#4CAF50), Blue (#2196F3), Amber (#FFC107)

**Key Attributes**: Trustworthy, Modern, Friendly, Professional, Memorable
