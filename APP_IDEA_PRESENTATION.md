
# Wasel - Travel & Delivery Connection App

## The Core Idea

**Wasel** connects two types of users:

1. **Travelers** - People who are traveling and have extra luggage space
2. **Senders** - People who need to send small items (documents, packages) to another location

Instead of using expensive courier services, senders can connect with travelers going to their destination. Travelers can earn money by utilizing their unused luggage capacity.

---

## User Workflow

### For Travelers (Carriers):

**Step 1: Post a Trip**
- Traveler opens the app and posts their upcoming trip
- They enter:
  - Departure city
  - Destination city
  - Travel date
  - Available luggage weight (e.g., 5 kg)
  - Delivery fee they want to charge

**Step 2: Receive Requests**
- Senders who need items delivered to that destination can send requests
- Traveler receives notifications about new delivery requests
- Each request shows:
  - Item description
  - Weight
  - Pickup and delivery locations
  - Sender's profile and rating

**Step 3: Accept or Decline**
- Traveler reviews each request
- Can accept requests that fit their schedule and luggage capacity
- Can communicate with sender to coordinate details

**Step 4: Coordinate & Deliver**
- Meet sender to collect the item
- Travel with the item
- Deliver to recipient at destination
- Both parties confirm successful delivery

---

### For Senders:

**Step 1: Search for Travelers**
- Sender opens the app and searches for travelers
- Filters by:
  - Destination city
  - Travel date
  - Available weight capacity
  - Price range
  - Traveler ratings

**Step 2: View Traveler Profiles**
- Browse list of travelers going to their destination
- Each profile shows:
  - Traveler name and photo
  - Rating and number of completed trips
  - Route (departure â†’ destination)
  - Travel date
  - Available weight
  - Delivery fee
  - Verification status

**Step 3: Send Request**
- Select a traveler
- Send delivery request with:
  - Item description
  - Weight
  - Pickup location
  - Delivery location
  - Any special instructions

**Step 4: Wait for Acceptance**
- Traveler reviews and accepts/declines the request
- If accepted, sender receives notification
- Sender makes payment through the app

**Step 5: Coordinate & Track**
- Communicate with traveler to arrange pickup
- Hand over item to traveler
- Track delivery status
- Confirm receipt when item is delivered

## Simple User Journey Example

**Scenario:** Sarah in New York needs to send documents to her client in London.

1. Sarah opens Wasel and searches for travelers going to London
2. She finds John, who is flying to London next week with 5kg available space
3. Sarah views John's profile - he has 4.8 stars and 15 completed trips
4. Sarah sends a request: "Legal documents, 0.5kg, pickup from Manhattan office, deliver to client in Central London"
5. John receives the request and accepts it
6. Sarah gets notification and pays $50 through the app
7. They coordinate via in-app messaging
8. John picks up documents from Sarah's office
9. John travels to London with the documents
10. John delivers to Sarah's client in London
11. Both confirm successful delivery
12. John receives payment, both users rate each other
